Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really free?
Yes, 100% free with no hidden charges, no watermarks, and no signup required.
What DPI should I choose?
150 DPI is ideal for screen viewing. Choose 300 DPI for print-quality output. 72 DPI is best for web thumbnails with smallest file size.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server?
No. All conversion happens entirely in your browser using PDF.js. Your file is never sent to any server.
Can I convert only specific pages?
Yes! Use the Custom Range option and enter pages like "1-3, 5, 7" to convert only selected pages.
What is the maximum PDF size?
You can process PDFs up to 200 MB. For larger files, we recommend splitting the PDF first.
