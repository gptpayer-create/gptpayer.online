Frequently Asked Questions

Does it support .doc files (older Word format)?
The tool works best with .docx (the modern format). Older .doc files may have limited support — we recommend converting to .docx first using Word or LibreOffice.
Will my tables and images be included?
Tables are fully supported and rendered in the PDF. Embedded images in the docx will be included if the browser can decode them. Complex image layouts may have minor formatting differences.
Is my document sent to a server?
No. Your .docx file is processed entirely in your browser using the open-source Mammoth.js library. It never leaves your device.
What is the maximum file size?
The tool handles files up to 50 MB comfortably. Very large files with many embedded images may take a few seconds longer to process.
Will fonts look the same as in Word?
The PDF uses web-safe fonts as fallbacks (Times New Roman, Arial). Custom fonts embedded in the .docx may render as their closest equivalent. For pixel-perfect results, Microsoft Word's export function is recommended.
