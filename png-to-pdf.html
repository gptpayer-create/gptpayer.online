About PNG to PDF Conversion

PNG is the ideal format for screenshots, diagrams, UI mockups, logos, and any image with sharp text or transparent backgrounds. Converting PNG files to PDF preserves the lossless quality of PNG while creating a universally shareable document format.

Why PNG for Documents?

Unlike JPG, PNG uses lossless compression — every pixel is preserved exactly. This makes PNG the superior choice for scanned text documents, software screenshots, charts, and diagrams where sharp edges and readable text are critical. When you convert PNG screenshots to PDF, the text stays perfectly crisp at any zoom level.

Transparency Support

PNG files can have transparent backgrounds — something JPG cannot support. Our converter lets you choose what happens to transparent areas: fill with white (best for printing), fill with a custom color, or keep the transparency in the PDF layer. This is particularly useful for logos, watermarks, and design elements.

Auto Orientation

The Auto Orientation feature detects whether each uploaded PNG is landscape or portrait and sets the page orientation accordingly. This is useful when you have a mixed batch of wide and tall images — each page will be correctly oriented without manual adjustment.

Privacy

Like all GPTPayer.online tools, PNG to PDF conversion happens entirely in your browser using jsPDF. Your files are never transmitted to any server. This is especially important for PNG screenshots that may contain sensitive information — account details, personal data, confidential work.
