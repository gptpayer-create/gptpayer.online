About Us

Who we are and why we built this tool

GPTPayer.online was created with a single, clear goal: to give everyone a fast, private, and completely free way to convert JPG images into PDF documents — without the hassle of registrations, subscriptions, or watermarks.

We are a small team of developers and designers who were frustrated with bloated, ad-heavy, slow online tools that required account signups just to do something as simple as combining images into a PDF. So we built something better.

Our Mission

We believe that simple productivity tools should be accessible to everyone, everywhere. Whether you're a student scanning notes, a professional compiling photos, a freelancer sending invoices, or simply someone who needs to send a photo as a PDF — GPTPayer.online gets the job done in seconds.

We are committed to keeping this tool free forever. We sustain the service through non-intrusive advertising only. We will never charge users, add watermarks, or sell your data.

How It Works

Unlike many online converters, GPTPayer.online does all processing directly in your web browser using modern JavaScript (powered by the open-source jsPDF library). This means your images are never uploaded to any server. Conversion is instant, private, and completely local to your device.

This approach also means GPTPayer.online works offline — once the page is loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and still convert your images to PDF.

What We Offer

  • Batch JPG to PDF conversion — combine multiple images into one PDF
  • Customizable page size: A4, Letter, A3
  • Portrait and landscape orientation support
  • Adjustable margins and custom filename
  • Zero watermarks on the final PDF
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers
  • No file size limits imposed by our servers
  • Works offline once the page is loaded
